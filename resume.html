Skills (the most important stuff)

I'm most proud of my project called GitHUD.

It's an open-source project for viewing GitHub repository and user statistics in a meaningful way. After entering GitHub usernames, each user's repos are displayed as individual 'cards' of sorts, containing two main charts: a donut chart of contributors and their amount of contribution, as well as a line chart showing the weekly number of commits over the life of the repo. The full screen view (still under development) will provide an extended amount of information for the repo.
